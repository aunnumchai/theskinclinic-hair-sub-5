### Image Specifications:
- **Recommended size**: 600x600 pixels
- **Format**: JPG
- **Quality**: High quality for lightbox viewing
- **Content**: Patient reviews and testimonials from the Mission Impossible campaign

### How to Add Images:
1. Copy the review images to the `/images/` folder
2. Ensure they are named exactly as listed above
3. Refresh the webpage to see them in the carousel

### Features:
- ✅ Auto-scrolling horizontal carousel
- ✅ Smooth infinite loop animation
- ✅ Hover to pause
- ✅ Click any image to view in lightbox (popup)
